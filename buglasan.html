BUGLASAN FESTIVAL 2023 – STREET DANCING & SHOWDOWN

The Buglasan Festival 2023 Street Dancing & Showdown were one of the most anticipated events of this year’s festival of festivals. 10 municipalities of Negros Oriental participated and made the job of the judges not easy. While over the last decades the showdown started in the morning at Lamberto L. Macias Sports & Cultural Complex followed by the street dancing in the afternoon, the organizers decided to change the successful concept. 2023 the street dancing was scheduled for 2:00 pm in the afternoon, followed by the showdown at 5:00 pm.

Buglasan Festival 2023 – Street Dancing

Announced and scheduled for 2:00 pm thousands of Negrosanons and guests from all over the world were waiting along the streets of Dumaguete City to witness the Street Dancing competition of the Buglasan Festival 2023. However, they were roasting in the sun, as, despite very experienced organizers, the people in charge could not keep the schedule up. The Buglasan Street Dancing finally started around 3:00 pm. The street dancing parade made its way along the Rizal Boulevard, through downtown Dumaguete to head back to Freedom Park.

SANDUROT FESTIVAL STREET DANCING PARADE

After the gathering and the opening show, it was time to finally start the street dancing parade. For this year’s street dancing music, the organizers thought it would be best to have the same festival music (on speakers) to be played during their parade.

Although it makes sense to have the uniform music for a competition, hearing it twice or thrice is enough for a single festival song. Disregarding the music, the bystanders and spectators were watching the parade regardless of the music loop. The colorful festival costumes and graceful dancing took the music to another level; and was quite enjoyable.

Buglasan Festival 2023 – Showdown

With the delay at the start of the day, the showdown was also pushed back. That didn´t lessen the energy of the dancers, which simply did a great job after already a long day of waiting and dancing. This year´s showdown venue also had a huge screen on one end. As only a few contingents utilized it in a way, it benefits their performance, the main purpose of the huge screen was showing VIPs and sponsors, while taking away a lot of seats for the audience. Utilized better, it certainly would add to the Buglasan Showdown experience.
